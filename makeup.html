    There are many theories about makeup products, including color theory, HD makeup, and contouring and highlighting:

HD makeup High definition makeup is known for its light diffusing properties, which can make it a good choice for photoshoots and weddings. Contouring and highlighting Applying contour and highlight strategically can create the illusion of different face shapes and enhance features.
